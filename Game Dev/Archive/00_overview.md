# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Overview

## Introduction

The World Tree trembles. Shadows lengthen across the Nine Realms. Ancient evils stir in forgotten places, and the balance that has held for millennia threatens to collapse.

Shadows of the Nine Realms is a cooperative tactical adventure board game for 1-4 players set in the rich tapestry of Nordic mythology. Players take on the roles of legendary heroes drawn from across the Nine Realms, fighting together against the encroaching darkness that threatens to unravel Yggdrasil itself.

## Design Pillars

- **Fail-forward:** Even low rolls accomplish something. No action should feel like a dead end.
- **Authentic Norse Folklore:** Creatures and lore drawn from actual Scandinavian mythology, not generic fantasy.
- **Portability and Fast Setup:** Book-box design, under 5 minutes to set up.
- **Cooperative, not competitive:** Players work together against the game.

## Key Features

- **D20 Dice Combat:** Streamlined dice system where each Character Board's Dice Table determines every outcome.
- **Fail Forward Mechanics:** Every roll moves the story forward.
- **1-9 Level Progression:** Traditional RPG-style progression with nine levels of growth.
- **Portable Book-Box Design:** Compact A4-sized box that opens like a book.
- **Authentic Norse Folklore:** Huldra, Nokken, Draugr, and more — each with authentic abilities and behaviors.

## Player Info

- **Players:** 1-4
- **Duration:** 45-90 minutes
- **Age:** 14+

## The Nine Realms

The World Tree Yggdrasil connects nine distinct realms, each with its own inhabitants, dangers, and mysteries:

| Realm | Description |
|---|---|
| **Asgard** | Realm of the Aesir gods, shining citadels and divine power. Home to Odin, Thor, and the halls of Valhalla. |
| **Midgard** | The mortal realm, where humanity struggles to survive against monsters, harsh winters, and the whims of gods. |
| **Jotunheim** | Land of frost and fire giants, treacherous and wild. Ancient enemies of both gods and mortals. |
| **Niflheim** | Realm of ice, mist, and primordial cold. Where the river Hvergelmir flows and the dragon Nidhogg gnaws at Yggdrasil's roots. |
| **Muspelheim** | Fire realm of the fire giants, burning and eternal. Home to Surtr, who will bring flame at Ragnarok. |
| **Alfheim** | Home of the light elves, beautiful and deceiving. A realm of magic and wonder. |
| **Svartalfheim** | Underground realm of dwarves and dark elves. Master craftsmen who forge legendary artifacts. |
| **Vanaheim** | Realm of the Vanir gods, nature and magic. Older than the Aesir, with their own ancient wisdom. |
| **Helheim** | Realm of the dead, ruled by the goddess Hel. Where those who die dishonorably find their rest. |
