# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Campaign System

The campaign spans multiple scenarios with persistent progression, story choices, and evolving challenges. Your choices determine which scenarios you face and what allies or enemies you make along the way.

---

## Campaign Structure

A standard campaign consists of **6-8 scenarios** played across multiple sessions. Your party's choices determine which scenarios you face.

- **Starting:** All heroes begin at Level 1 with starting abilities
- **Between Scenarios:** Recover health, spend loot, level up if enough XP
- **Story Choices:** Record decisions in the Campaign Journal — they matter

## Loot & Equipment

Loot Tokens represent equipment, artifacts, and resources gathered during adventures. Spend loot between scenarios:

| Item | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Healing Potions | 3 loot | Restore 3 health |
| Upgrade Weapons | 5 loot | +1 damage on all attacks |
| Learn New Abilities | 8 loot | Gain a 5th ability card |
| Blessing of the Gods | 10 loot | Start next scenario with 2 Fate Tokens |

## Story Tracking

The Campaign Journal tracks persistent story elements:

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| **ALLIES** | Characters who owe you favors (Huldra, Dwarf Smiths, etc.) |
| **ENEMIES** | Factions you've angered (Frost Giants, Corrupted Spirits, etc.) |
| **LEGACY** | Permanent bonuses earned through choices |
| **SECRETS** | Clues discovered that may unlock hidden scenarios |
