# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Combat System

## The D20 System

When you perform an attack, roll a D20 and consult your character board's Dice Result Table. Each class has unique thresholds and effects based on their specialty.

---

## Fail Forward Mechanics

Unlike traditional games where low rolls result in complete failure, Shadows of the Nine Realms uses a "fail forward" philosophy. Even poor rolls accomplish something, ensuring the action never stalls.

| Roll | Result | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1-3 | Glancing Blow | Deal 1 damage OR minor bonus effect |
| 4-6 | Weak Hit | Half damage (round down), no bonus effects |
| 7-14 | Standard Hit | Full damage/effect, normal bonuses |
| 15-19 | Strong Hit | Full damage + bonus effect OR Fate Token |
| 20 | Critical Hit | Double damage + all bonuses + 2 Fate Tokens |

---

## Fate Tokens

Fate Tokens represent the Norns' favor and can be spent to influence destiny. You can hold a **maximum of 3 Fate Tokens** at any time.

### Spend 1 Fate Token to:
- Reroll any single D20 (must use new result)
- Add +2 to a D20 result after rolling
- Negate 1 Exhaustion you would suffer
- Move 1 additional hex as part of any movement
