## Draugr — The Undead

*"The dead walk when dishonored. The Draugr remembers life… and hates the living for it."*

Draugr are undead Vikings who rise from their graves to torment the living. Unlike mindless zombies, they retain intelligence and malice. They guard their burial treasures with unholy fury.
