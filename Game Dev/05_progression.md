# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Level Progression

Heroes gain Experience (XP) across a campaign and grow in power through 9 levels. Progression is designed so a strong campaign (6 wins, 2 losses across 8 scenarios) reaches Level 9 naturally.

---

## XP Sources

XP comes from two sources, both always active regardless of session outcome.

### 1. Scenario XP
Awarded at Act 3 Resolution:

| Outcome | Scenario XP | Additional Reward |
|---|---|---|
| Win | 20 XP | Full loot as defined by Scenario Sleeve |
| Loss | 10 XP | 1 Fate Token each + named consolation reward (printed on Scenario Sleeve) |

Heroes gain experience even in defeat — every battle makes them wiser. The consolation reward is always specific and thematic, not a generic consolation prize.

### 2. Card XP
Certain ability card actions are marked with an **XP icon** (a runic symbol). These icons appear on actions that reflect what the class is *supposed to do* — playing into your class identity earns XP.

When you trigger an XP-marked action, place 1 XP Token on your character board immediately. At Act 3, total your tokens and add to your running XP.

**Expected card XP per session:** 8-12 XP (roughly 3-4 triggers across 7-10 rounds).

Each class has 3-4 XP-marked actions defined in Phase 2. See `04_classes.md`.

---

## XP Thresholds & Rewards

| Level | XP Needed | XP Gap | Rewards |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | — | Starting board, starting card pool, 3 equipment slots unlocked |
| 2 | 25 | 25 | Unlock ability card (pool grows) |
| 3 | 55 | 30 | +2 Health |
| 4 | 90 | 35 | Signature ability enhanced |
| 5 | 120 | 30 | Dice threshold improvement (Strong Hit: 14-19) |
| 6 | 150 | 30 | Unlock ability card (pool grows) |
| 7 | 180 | 30 | +2 Health |
| 8 | 210 | 30 | Signature ability mastery |
| 9 | 240 | 30 | **Saga Hero** — dice threshold improved (Strong Hit: 13-19) |

### Campaign Math
- **Strong campaign (6W / 2L):** 6×20 + 2×10 + 8 sessions × ~10 card XP = ~220 XP → **reaches Level 9**
- **Tough campaign (4W / 4L):** 4×20 + 4×10 + 8 sessions × ~10 card XP = ~200 XP → **reaches Level 8**

---

## Card Pool Growth

Each class has a defined maximum card pool. Cards are unlocked at **Level 2** and **Level 6** through the progression track. No other source adds ability cards — variety between sessions comes from item equipment, not card unlocks.

| Level | Cards Unlocked | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Starting pool (class-defined: 6 or 8) | Varies by class |
| 2 | +2 | First unlock |
| 4 | +2 | Second unlock |
| 6 | +2 | Third unlock |
| 8 | +2 | Fourth unlock |
| 9 | No new cards | Saga Hero |

Maximum pool at Level 9: **10 cards** (small pool classes: Ulfhednar) to **16 cards** (large pool classes: all others). Hand size varies by class — from 4 to 6 cards selected per Act. See `04_classes.md` for per-class values.

Classes are designed so that even at maximum pool, hand selection requires genuine sacrifice each Act. The TOP+BOTTOM pairing rule means a hand of 6 cards offers up to 30 distinct pairing combinations per round.

## Dice Threshold Progression

| Level Range | Standard Hit | Strong Hit | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-4 (Base) | 7-14 | 15-19 | 20 |
| 5-8 (Tier 1) | 7-13 | **14-19** | 20 |
| 9 (Legendary) | 7-12 | **13-19** | 20 |

> The Character Board must indicate which threshold tier is active. See Phase 7.

## Level 9 — Saga Hero

Reaching Level 9 unlocks **Saga Hero** status. The hero can no longer earn XP. Any action that would trigger a card XP icon instead generates a **Saga Point** (use XP tokens, different meaning).

### Saga Points
Spent to directly benefit co-players:

| Cost | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 Saga Point | Give 1 Fate Token to any hero |
| 2 Saga Points | Any hero moves +2 hexes this turn |
| 3 Saga Points | Any hero negates damage from one attack |
| 4 Saga Points | Any hero immediately takes a bonus turn |

## Equipment Slots

Every hero has **3 equipment slots** on their Character Board from Level 1:

| Slot | Type | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Weapon | Affects attack behaviour | Runed Blade, Spear of Precision, Venom Edge |
| Armour | Affects defence and resilience | Iron Shield, Wolf Pelt, Rune-Stitched Cloak |
| Relic | Affects special abilities and passives | Blessing of the Gods, Rune-Carved Pendant, Cloak of Shadows |

One item per slot. Swapping equipment happens **between scenarios only** — never mid-session. Items are purchased with Loot Tokens at the loot shop.

---

## Loot Shop

Loot Tokens are the universal currency earned from scenarios, objectives, and class actions. Spent between scenarios. Individual per hero — maximum 10 Loot Tokens held at any time.

### Consumables
Single-use. Disappear after use.

| Item | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Healing Draught | 2 Loot | Restore 3 Health (use once, any session) |
| Mead of Courage | 3 Loot | Gain 2 Fate Tokens immediately |
| Rune Salve | 3 Loot | Remove all Exhaustion Tokens (use once, any session) |

### Weapon Equipment
One equipped at a time. Replaces previous Weapon item (replaced item is discarded).

| Item | Cost | Effect | Lore Echo |
|---|---|---|---|
| Runed Blade | 4 Loot | Whenever you hit an enemy, deal 1 damage to an adjacent enemy | — |
| Spear of Precision | 6 Loot | Your Glancing Blows (1-3) deal 2 damage instead of 1 | Gungnir — never misses |
| Venom Edge | 5 Loot | Enemies you hit gain the Wound condition | — |

### Armour Equipment
One equipped at a time.

| Item | Cost | Effect | Lore Echo |
|---|---|---|---|
| Iron Shield | 3 Loot | Gain Shield 1 at the start of each Act | — |
| Wolf Pelt | 4 Loot | Gain 1 Fate Token whenever you suffer Exhaustion from a monster attack | Ulfhednar wolf-shirt |
| Rune-Stitched Cloak | 5 Loot | Reduce all incoming damage by 1 (minimum 1) | — |

### Relic Equipment
One equipped at a time.

| Item | Cost | Effect | Lore Echo |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blessing of the Gods | 6 Loot | Strained threshold permanently moves to 4 tokens | — |
| Rune-Carved Pendant | 5 Loot | Gain 1 Fate Token whenever a cursed or Wounded enemy is defeated | — |
| Cloak of Shadows | 6 Loot | Start each Act with Invisible for 1 round | — |
| Draupnir Echo | 7 Loot | Gain 1 Loot Token at the end of each scenario automatically | Draupnir — the ring that multiplies |

> **Realm Campaign Packs** introduce realm-specific items to the shop — Jotunheim adds frost-themed relics, Svartalfheim adds dwarf-forged weapons. The item layer stays fresh across the grand campaign without touching class card design.
