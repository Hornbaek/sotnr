# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Level Progression

As you complete scenarios, your heroes gain Experience (XP) and grow in power. The progression system spans nine levels, each bringing new abilities and improvements.

## XP & Rewards Table

| Level | XP Needed | Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | Starting board, 4 ability cards |
| 2 | 20 | Unlock 5th ability card |
| 3 | 50 | +2 Health, improved dice threshold |
| 4 | 90 | Signature ability enhanced |
| 5 | 140 | +2 Health, improved dice threshold |
| 6 | 200 | Unlock 6th ability card |
| 7 | 270 | +2 Health, improved dice threshold |
| 8 | 350 | Signature ability mastery |
| 9 | 450 | Legendary hero — all thresholds improved |
