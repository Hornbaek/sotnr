# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Quick Reference

## D20 Result Table

| Roll | Result |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | Glancing Blow — Minimum effect |
| 4-6 | Weak Hit — Half damage, no bonus |
| 7-14 | Standard Hit — Full damage/effect |
| 15-19 | Strong Hit — Full damage + bonus OR Fate |
| 20 | Critical Hit — Double damage + 2 Fate |

---

## Fate Token Spending

- Reroll any D20 (must use new result)
- Add +2 to a D20 after rolling
- Negate 1 Exhaustion
- Move +1 hex as part of any movement

**Maximum: 3 Fate Tokens per hero**

---

## Turn Order

**1. HERO PHASE:**
Players take turns (any order, lowest initiative first). Play 2 cards (Top + Bottom, any order).

**2. MONSTER PHASE:**
Activate by spawn number (1-4). Determine Target (preferred keyword) → Move toward target → Attack if in range → Apply special rules.

---

## Movement

| Situation | Effect |
|---|---|
| Normal Terrain | 1 movement point per hex |
| Difficult Terrain | 2 movement points per hex |
| Flanking (behind target) | +2 to attack roll |
| Higher Ground | +1 to attack roll |
| Obstacle | Blocks line of sight and movement |

---

## Conditions

| Condition | Effect |
|---|---|
| Wound | Suffer 1 damage at start of each turn |
| Poison | Suffer 1 damage; healing reduced by 1 |
| Immobilize | Cannot perform Move actions |
| Disarm | Cannot perform Attack actions |
| Stun | Skip your entire turn |
| Muddle | All attacks roll at -2 |
| Strengthen | All attacks roll at +2 |
| Invisible | Cannot be targeted |
| Shield X | Reduce next X damage by X |
