# Shadows of the Nine Realms — Scenarios

Each scenario uses a Scenario Sleeve (front/back design) that slides over the map half of the book-box. The sleeve defines everything needed to run the scenario — terrain, objectives, Act triggers, transitions, and Legacy Card instructions.

## Scenario Sleeve Format

Every sleeve is structured across two sides with seven zones:

**Front Side:**
- **Zone 1 — Scenario Header:** Name, location, difficulty, recommended players
- **Zone 2 — Hex Map Overlay:** Terrain, starting positions, monster spawn hexes, objective markers
- **Zone 3 — Act 1:** Trigger type, round count (if applicable), Victorious outcome, Besieged outcome
- **Zone 6 — Special Rules:** General scenario-wide rules

**Back Side:**
- **[Act 1 → Act 2 Transition Strip]:** Recovery suppression icons, hand reset suppression icons (if any)
- **Zone 4 — Act 2:** Trigger type, round count (if applicable), Victorious outcome, Besieged outcome
- **[Act 2 → Act 3 Transition Strip]:** Recovery suppression icons, hand reset suppression icons (if any)
- **Zone 5 — Act 3 Resolution:** Win text, loss text, consolation reward, Legacy Card instruction

### Act Trigger Types
- **Type A (Objective):** Act ends when a condition is met by either side
- **Type B (Round Count):** Act ends after X rounds regardless of what happened
- **Type C (Survival):** Act ends after X rounds — outcome depends on whether a condition was avoided

Every Act has one primary trigger and one fallback trigger. Every transition resolves into exactly two named outcomes: **Victorious** or **Besieged**.
